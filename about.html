About Our Dining

Good Food Good Life!

VieVie’s Indian street food is a homegrown brand created to fulfill the craving for good quality health and original Indian food true to its roots. Our influence is taken from the traditional Indian recipes by our mothers and grandmothers and developed into a unique contemporary menu with a hint of nostalgia. With a twist to the classics, certain dishes will take you to the streets of Mumbai, while others will open your eyes and taste buds to something new yet familiar.

We didn’t want to reinvent this distinctive cuisine; rather than reimagine it. We are serving you up fresh, healthy food and timeless words of wisdom. Our aim is to make the menu more approachable by simplifying and modernizing popular classic dishes. We have updated with selected dishes wish list retaining the unique Indian flavors.
